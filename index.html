The Home Reality

Indian homes frequently suffer from harsh threshold gradients, acoustic pollution, and poor circulation. These structural defaults exert continuous neurobiological stress, reversing recovery.

The Methodology

Design Behaviour Codes

DBCs operationalize cognitive science into spatial metrics. Central to this is the Two-Brain Logic: environments dictate whether a patient is locked in reactive survival mode (System 1) or can access deliberative healing (System 2).

  • 1

    Sensory Load Calibration

    Managing the autonomic nervous system by controlling environmental stimuli to prevent cognitive override.

  • 2

    Predictable Modulation

    Using measurable thresholds (circulation, rhythm) to predictably shift neurobiological states.

Operationalizing the Vision

Deploying trained teams to execute rapid, low-cost architectural interventions based on precise DBC parameters directly in post-discharge residential spaces.

Partnership & Prescription

The service integrates with hospital EHR systems. Upon discharge for critical conditions (e.g., cardiac, post-op), physicians formally "prescribe" a DBC Home Assessment based on the patient's sensitivity profile.
